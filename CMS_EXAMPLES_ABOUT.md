# About Page Content Example

Create a new page in Admin Dashboard → CMS with these details:

## Page Settings
- **Slug:** `about`
- **Title:** `About Aideesigns & Stitches`

## Content Blocks to Add

### Block 1: Introduction (Text Block)
**Block Key:** `intro`
**Block Type:** `text`
**Content:**
```
Welcome to Aideesigns & Stitches, where elegance meets craftsmanship. Since our founding, we've been dedicated to creating bespoke outfits that celebrate your unique style and personality.

Our journey began with a simple belief: everyone deserves to wear clothing that makes them feel confident, beautiful, and authentically themselves. Today, we're proud to serve customers across Nigeria with our ready-to-wear collections and custom tailoring services.
```

### Block 2: Our Story (Text Block)
**Block Key:** `our_story`
**Block Type:** `text`
**Content:**
```
THE AIDEESIGNS STORY

What started as a passion for fashion and design has blossomed into a thriving atelier dedicated to creating exceptional outfits. Our founder's vision was to make high-quality, custom fashion accessible to everyone, not just the elite.

Every piece we create tells a story - your story. Whether it's a wedding dress for your special day, a corporate suit for that important meeting, or traditional attire for a cultural celebration, we pour our heart and expertise into every stitch.
```

### Block 3: Our Values (Text Block)
**Block Key:** `values`
**Block Type:** `text`
**Content:**
```
WHAT WE STAND FOR

Quality Craftsmanship
We use only premium fabrics and materials, ensuring every garment stands the test of time.

Personal Touch
Your vision drives our process. We listen, advise, and create pieces that exceed your expectations.

Timeless Elegance
Our designs blend contemporary trends with classic sophistication, ensuring you look stunning today and years from now.

Customer First
Your satisfaction is our priority. From consultation to final fitting, we're with you every step of the way.
```

### Block 4: Our Process (Text Block)
**Block Key:** `process`
**Block Type:** `text`
**Content:**
```
HOW WE WORK

1. Consultation
   Share your vision, style preferences, and the occasion. We'll guide you through fabric choices and design options.

2. Design & Measurement
   Our expert tailors take precise measurements and create detailed sketches of your custom piece.

3. Creation
   Watch your outfit come to life in our atelier. We maintain open communication throughout the process.

4. Fitting & Adjustments
   Try on your garment and we'll make any necessary adjustments to ensure a perfect fit.

5. Final Delivery
   Receive your stunning outfit, ready to make you shine at your event.
```

### Block 5: Call to Action (Text Block)
**Block Key:** `cta`
**Block Type:** `text`
**Content:**
```
READY TO CREATE SOMETHING BEAUTIFUL?

Whether you're browsing our ready-to-wear collection or dreaming of a custom creation, we're here to bring your vision to life. Book a consultation today and let's start your fashion journey together.
```

---

## How to Add in Admin Dashboard

1. Go to **Admin Dashboard** → **CMS**
2. Click **"Create Page"**
3. Enter:
   - Slug: `about`
   - Title: `About Aideesigns & Stitches`
4. Click **"Create Page"**
5. Click **"Edit Content"** on the new page
6. For each block above:
   - Click **"Add Block"**
   - Enter the Block Key
   - Select Block Type: `text`
   - Paste the content
   - Click **"Add Block"**
7. Visit **http://localhost:3000/about** to see your page!
