# Privacy Policy Content Example

Create a new page in Admin Dashboard → CMS with these details:

## Page Settings
- **Slug:** `privacy`
- **Title:** `Privacy Policy`

## Content Blocks to Add

### Block 1: Introduction (Text Block)
**Block Key:** `intro`
**Block Type:** `text`
**Content:**
```
Last Updated: February 14, 2026

Aideesigns & Stitches ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site.
```

### Block 2: Information We Collect (Text Block)
**Block Key:** `information_collection`
**Block Type:** `text`
**Content:**
```
INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Personal Information
When you place an order, make a booking, or contact us, we may collect:
- Name and contact information (email, phone number, address)
- Payment information (processed securely through our payment partners)
- Measurements and style preferences for custom orders
- Photos you upload for inspiration

Automatically Collected Information
When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:
- Browser type and version
- IP address
- Pages you visit and time spent on pages
- Device information
- Referring website addresses
```

### Block 3: How We Use Your Information (Text Block)
**Block Key:** `information_use`
**Block Type:** `text`
**Content:**
```
HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect to:
- Process and fulfill your orders
- Communicate with you about your orders and bookings
- Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
- Send you updates about your orders (with your consent)
- Improve our website and services
- Prevent fraudulent transactions
- Comply with legal obligations
```

### Block 4: Information Sharing (Text Block)
**Block Key:** `information_sharing`
**Block Type:** `text`
**Content:**
```
SHARING YOUR INFORMATION

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information with:

Service Providers
- Payment processors (Paystack, Flutterwave) to process transactions
- Delivery services to fulfill orders
- Email service providers to send order confirmations

Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or if we believe such action is necessary to:
- Comply with legal processes
- Protect our rights and property
- Prevent fraud or security issues
- Protect the safety of our customers
```

### Block 5: Data Security (Text Block)
**Block Key:** `data_security`
**Block Type:** `text`
**Content:**
```
DATA SECURITY

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

These measures include:
- Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption for data transmission
- Secure servers and databases
- Regular security assessments
- Limited access to personal information by employees

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
```

### Block 6: Your Rights (Text Block)
**Block Key:** `your_rights`
**Block Type:** `text`
**Content:**
```
YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:
- Access the personal information we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
- Withdraw consent for marketing communications
- Object to processing of your information

To exercise these rights, please contact us at hello@aideesigns.com
```

### Block 7: Cookies (Text Block)
**Block Key:** `cookies`
**Block Type:** `text`
**Content:**
```
COOKIES AND TRACKING

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:
- Remember your preferences
- Understand how you use our website
- Improve your browsing experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.
```

### Block 8: Children's Privacy (Text Block)
**Block Key:** `children_privacy`
**Block Type:** `text`
**Content:**
```
CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
```

### Block 9: Contact Information (Text Block)
**Block Key:** `contact`
**Block Type:** `text`
**Content:**
```

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: hello@aideesigns.com
Phone: +234 XXX XXX XXXX
Address: Lagos, Nigeria

We will respond to your inquiry within 7 business days.
```

---

## How to Add in Admin Dashboard

1. Go to **Admin Dashboard** → **CMS**
2. Click **"Create Page"**
3. Enter:
   - Slug: `privacy`
   - Title: `Privacy Policy`
4. Click **"Create Page"**
5. Click **"Edit Content"** on the new page
6. Add each block listed above
7. Visit **http://localhost:3000/privacy** to see your page!
